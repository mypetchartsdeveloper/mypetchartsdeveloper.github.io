My Pet Charts Privacy Policy

My Pet Charts does not collect/distribute/sell any information provided to the application.

All information provided to the application is stored on the device that it was entered into.

My Pet Charts needs access to the following on your device:

  • Phone: This allows My Pet Charts to place a phone call to the provided veterinary phone number.
  • SMS: This allows My Pet Charts to send SMS messages when you choose to share appointments.
  • Contacts: Allows you to select a contact to send an SMS to when sharing appointments.
  • Files and Media: Allows you to select a picture to place on your pets chart.
  • My Pet Charts also has the ability to open your maps application to allow you to navigate to the veterinary address provided. It does not have the ability to track your location.

For questions about My Pet Charts or this policy contact: mypetchartsdeveloper@gmail.com.
